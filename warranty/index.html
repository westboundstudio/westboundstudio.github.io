Warranty

I take pride in my workmanship and want you to enjoy your jewelry for many years. For this reason, I guarantee my work for one year after the purchase date. I will repair any manufacturing defects free of charge during that time. Please contact me at info@westbound.studio to receive a prepaid shipping label.

Please note that theft, mysterious disappearance, loss, damage, or normal wear and tear (such as scratches) are not covered under this warranty.

To keep your jewelry in the best condition, I recommend that you have it cleaned and inspected by a qualified jeweler every 6 months.

I also recommend having all your fine jewelry insured through your homeowners or rental policy.
