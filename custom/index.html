Custom

Whether you have old jewelry you'd like to reinvent or nothing but a great idea, I can help make your vision a reality. I have several years of experience making custom jewelry that will last for generations. Working with a client to make their dream jewelry is a very special and rewarding experience.

Using any information and images you send me, I'll work to create renderings and an estimate for you within a few days. After your approval, it will take about 2 weeks for me to ship you the finished product.

If you have a project you'd like me to design and create for you, please contact me using the form below or email me at info@westbound.studio.
